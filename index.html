About Stellar

Dedicated to the
Deep Sky Pursuits

Founded in 2024, Stellar has grown from a handful of backyard astronomers into a massive global community. We are committed to making space accessible to everyone, regardless of their equipment or knowledge level.

  • Global network of dark-sky observation sites
  • Expert mentorship for novice stargazers
  • Professional astrophotography workshops
