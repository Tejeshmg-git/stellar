Elite Standards

Tips & Best Practices

Maintaining scientific integrity and equipment safety starts with professional conduct at our sites.

  • Red Light Protocol

    Preserve night vision. Use ONLY red light once on site. White light is strictly prohibited.

  • Cool-Down Management

    Allow your optics at least 60 minutes to reach thermal equilibrium for sharp images.

  • Equipment Safety

    Always use safety straps for cameras and counterweight bolts for mounts.
