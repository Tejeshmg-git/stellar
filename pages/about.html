Explorers of the Infinite

From humble backyard gazing to professional deep-space research, we are united by one passion: The Stars.

Our Story

From A Single Lens To A Galaxy of Minds

Stellar began in 2024 as a small gathering of five friends on a rooftop, sharing a single 4-inch refractor telescope. We faced the challenge of light pollution and limited resources, but our curiosity was boundless.

Today, we have grown into a vibrant community of over 1,200 members, managing our own dark-sky observational site and partnering with global institutions. Our journey proves that you don't need a PhD to contribute to science—just a willingness to look up.

Our Core Principle

Guided by scientific integrity and a passion for discovery.

Mission

To provide access to professional astronomical equipment and education, fostering a community where anyone can explore the universe.

Vision

A future where scientific literacy is universal, and the night sky is preserved as a heritage for generations to come.

Values

Inclusivity in science, preservation of dark skies, and mentorship for the next generation of astronomers.

The Next Horizon

Our future is as vast as the universe itself. In 2027, we aim to launch "StellarConnect", a network of remotely operated telescopes allowing members to capture images from the other side of the world, and break ground on our public Planetarium project.
