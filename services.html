Safety & Preparation

Ensuring a safe and enjoyable environment for all explorers.

Layer Up

Night temperatures at our observatories can drop 15-20F below city levels. Insulated boots and thermal layers are mandatory.

Red Light Only

To preserve night vision, only red-filtered flashlights are permitted on our observation decks. No bright white lights or flash photography.

Gear Maintenance

Rentals include a full power supply. However, we recommend bringing a portable power bank for long-exposure imaging sessions.
